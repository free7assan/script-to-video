GoScript — Logo Variants

G + Feather concept · 6 distinct directions

Continuous Flow

A single flowing G letterform with feather strokes emerging from the left side. The pen nib at the bottom-right anchors the writing theme. Organic curves suggest script writing and creativity.
OrganicMonolineWriting

Geometric Mark

Bold, structured G built from precise arcs and straight lines. Three parallel feather barbs on the left create a quill texture. Clean and authoritative — suits a professional SaaS brand.
GeometricBoldStructured

Feather Quill

The G is drawn as a single continuous curved stroke that resolves into a quill shaft at the bottom. Feather barbs radiate from the upper-right curve. The pen nib finishes the descender. Artsy and distinctive.
IllustrativeOrganicArtistic

Minimal Dot-Line

Ultra-minimal: the G starts as a dot (the quill tip viewed from above), transitions to an arc, crossbar, then nib. Dashed feather lines on the left suggest motion and lightness. Modern, app-icon friendly.
MinimalModernApp Icon

Split Stroke

Double parallel lines form the G, creating a sophisticated tech feel. Feather barbs extend from the left on both upper and lower sides. The split stroke suggests video editing tracks or dual timelines.
TechParallelEditorial

Badge / Crest

The G sits inside a rounded badge with a subtle border. Feather barbs form chevron-like patterns on the left, giving a premium, trust-mark feel. Works well as a favicon or app launcher icon.
BadgePremiumTrust

Feather Wing

The feather is expressed as wing-like curves fanning outward from the left side of the G. The upper barbs sweep up, lower barbs sweep down, creating a soaring feel. Evokes freedom and creative flow.
SoaringWingExpressive
